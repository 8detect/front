Minds Plus is a paid subscription that provides you with added benefits on Minds, across all mobile and web channels.

The benefits include:

  • Minds Plus badge on your channel
  • Option to request a Verified badge for your channel
  • Opt-out of all ads and boosted content on the site
  • Access to exclusive Minds content
  • Attend monthly hangouts with the Minds team

By signing up for Plus, users are given a Plus user badge on their channel.

In many cases, users will also want to verify their identity. Plus provides users with the option to request a Verified badge. Once a request is submitted, the Minds team will review the submission.

Minds users will notice that there are advertisements and boosted content on the site. The purpose of the boost queue is to provide our users with the ability to promote their content and reach new audiences. It also provides our users with a way to discover new content they enjoy, outside of who they are subscribed to.

With Plus, you are given the option to opt-out of the boost queue and all third party ads so that they do not appear on your feeds. While we hope that you continue to use the boost queue to keep a better pulse on the Minds community, we understand that many users only desire to see the content they are subscribed to. Plus lets you make this call.

Plus users will gain access to exclusive Minds content.
