The Minds Token

We are an open source and decentralized platform where users earn crypto for contributions to the community. Our goal is to provide a new model for content creators to take back their Internet freedom, monetization and social reach.

Building a contribution economy

We are building the worlds leading open source social network where you are rewarded for your contributions, not exploited.

Wire

The peer-to-peer payment system empowers you to receive one-time or recurring revenue from your supporters as tips or in exchange for customizable premium subscriptions and services.

Rewards

The reward system enables you to earn tokens for your daily contributions to the network. Tokens can be used throughout the full network.

Social networking on the blockchain

Minds Tokens are platform agnostic and leverage the Ethereum blockchain to reduce fees, improve advertising transparency and provide autonomy for digital content creators.
