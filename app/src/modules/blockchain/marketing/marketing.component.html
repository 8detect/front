Vision

Minds is building the world’s largest free and open source social network, where the users who contribute the most to the network will be rewarded for their efforts in cryptocurrency.

It’s here now.

The tech is live behind-the-scenes and will be immediately usable throughout the Minds network after the token distribution event.

Wire

The peer-to-peer payment system empowers you to receive one-time or recurring revenue from your supporters as tips or in exchange for customized premium subscriptions and services.

Boost

The surveillance-free ad network allows you to exchange your tokens for expanded reach and targeted promotion across the Minds network.

Rewards

The reward system enables you to earn tokens for your daily contributions to the network. Tokens can be used throughout the network or traded for other currencies on third-party exchanges.

Value

Minds tokens are platform agnostic and leverage the Ethereum blockchain to reduce fees, improve transparency and provide autonomy for digital content creators.
